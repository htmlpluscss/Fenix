Fenilux workflow

How it Works

  1. Two parties, the Client and the Contractor, sign a contract for services, goods, or freelance work.
  2. They specify Fenilux as their Payment Agent in the Payment Terms section of that contract.
  3. The Client, Contractor, and Fenilux sign a tripartite Agreement regulating the services of the Payment Agent.
  4. Fenilux undertakes to receive funds from the Client and transfer them to the Contractor.
  5. The additional services may include escrow, invoice processing and aggregation, and KYC procedures.
  6. Fenilux ensures smooth financial transactions, enhancing efficiency and reliability.
